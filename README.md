## Your PC isn't good enough to NASA Solar System?
[Try this!](https://alaanvv.github.io/Solar-System-3D/)

### Controls

 - `W, A, S, D, Scroll` - Move
 - `0, 1, 2, ..., 8` - Sun to Neptune
 - `R` - Reset your camera direction
 - `F` - Enable/Disable going faster
